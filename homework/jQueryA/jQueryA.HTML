Get two slices of bread, two tablespoons of butter, and three slices of cheese to your liking.
Butter the bread with a butter knife and then put the cheese between the two slices of bread.
Press the Step 3 button!
Place the sandwich on the pan and let cook for 3-5 minutes on each side.
Turn off the heat and using a spatula, place the completed sandwich on a plate, cut in half, and enjoy!
