Biography

Frieza is a major antagonist from Dragon Ball Z. Cruel and deadly, Frieza commands a force or thousands which conquers many planets in the universe which he then sells for profit. Frieza first appeared as the main antagonist of the Namek Saga and was the reason Goku became a Super Saiyan out of anger.

Special Moves

Death Beam: a purple beam of energy that emenates from Frieza's pointer finger. Frieza only needs to point at someone to fire this beam. The beam is very fast and difficult to dodge.
Death Ball: a ball of purple energy that Frieza uses to destroy planets. The ball is unstable as seen by sparks surrounding it. Frieza uses this technique to destroy Planet Namek.
Death Saucer: Two disks of energy which track their target. These disks are extremely sharp displaying their deadliness. Goku uses the tracking to his advantage and defeats Frieza by having the disks hit Frieza instead.

Transformations

Second Form: In this form Frieza grows bigger and becomes more muscular. His horns become longer and sharper and he becomes taller than all his other forms. This form resembles his father King Cold.
Third Form: In this form Frieza begins to hunch while his shoulders and head became way larger than his other forms. Interestingly, Akira Toriyama, the creator of Dragon Ball, based this form off the Xenomorph from Alien.
Final Form: This is the last form Frieza takes on Planet Namek when he faces off against Goku. This is also his true form as the other forms were only used to supress his power. In this form Frieza thins out, his skin becomes white and his head shines purple. Furthermore he is smaller than all his other forms.
