Gohan

Son Gohan

First Appearance

Dragon Ball Z Chapter 2 (1988)

Likes

Studying, Eating, His family

Dislikes

Fighting, Killing, Those who hurt his friends and family

Appears in

Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Super, Dragon Ball GT

Biography

Gohan is the son of Goku and is one of the most prominent characters in DBZ. As the son of Goku, Gohan is immensely powerful as seen in how he was the most powerful character at the end of the Cell and Buu Sagas. However, Gohan does not love to fight as his father does. Therefore, when his father came back to life, Gohan's training took a back seat and he became a scholar.

Special Moves

Kamehameha: A blue beam of energy that emenates from Gohan's hands. Gohan learned this technique from his father Goku and uses it prominently in the series.
Masenko: A yellow beam of energy similar to the Kamehameha. Gohan learned this from Piccolo and used this technique as a child before learning the Kamehameha.
Soaring Dragon Flight: A powerful uppercut technique. Gohan uses this when he first goes Super Saiyan 2 agaisnt cell.

Transformations

Super Saiyan: Goku teaches Gohan how to go Super Saiyan when training to defeat Cell. In this form Gohan has golden hair and green eyes. Furthermore his power is increased a hundred times over.
Super Saiyan 2: Gohan achieves this form after witnessing Android 16's sacrifice. In this form Gohan's hair becomes more spikey than his super saiyan form and he is hundreds of times more powerful than a normal super saiyan.
Ultimate Form: This is the form Gohan takes after he unlocks his potential with the Elder Kai. In this form, Gohan's hair is more spikey and he has a single bang over his face. Furthermore, this transformation is extremely powerful, even dominating Super Saiyan 2 in terms of power.
