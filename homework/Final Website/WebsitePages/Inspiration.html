    This page displays the websites I used as inspirations, my Wireframes, and my Creative Brief.

    Inspirations

    Apple: I liked how some of the websites used programs like pagePiling to change up the background and content of the page smoothly and effectively.
    Wikipedia: As my website is an Encylopedia for Dragon Ball, I figured Wikipedia was a good place to start off as an Inspiration. I liked how it would seperate its different articles into section which I used to distinguish sections such as Special moves and trasnformations.
    Fandom: This website acts as a home for enclopedias for many different works of media. Some of these enclopedias displayed things like "first appeared" and "Likes/Dislikes" under character icons and I thought that would be interesting to use for a Dragon Ball Enyclopedia. I also liked how when a picture is cliked, it blows up like fancybox.
    Youtube: I liked the way Youtube displayed its header and figured that I could use that on my website as well.
    Playstation: The home page of Playstation.com I used as reference when designing the character pages. I liked how it split up different sections of the Playstation Brand into different areas of the home page.
